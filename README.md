# EU AI Act & OWASP 2025 Compliance Checklists  
**Open-source templates for AI governance, risk assessment, and security audits**



## Overview
This repository provides free, open-source checklists, templates, and mapping guides to help developers, startups, and organizations achieve **EU AI Act compliance**, mitigate **OWASP LLM Top 10 vulnerabilities (2025 update)**, align with **NIST AI RMF**, and perform **generative AI security audits**.

Designed for high-risk AI systems, generative models (LLMs), and teams needing quick **AI compliance verification**, **red-teaming AI systems**, **prompt injection prevention**, **bias and fairness verification**, and **transparency obligations** fulfillment.

Supports multilingual workflows (English, Deutsch, Français + more) – ideal for EU-based teams in Germany, Hesse, and beyond.

## Key Features
- **EU AI Act Compliance Checklist** – Risk classification (Annex III), high-risk obligations (Articles 6–15), conformity assessment templates
- **OWASP LLM Vulnerabilities Mapping** – Covers prompt injection, training data poisoning, insecure output handling, bias exploits (OWASP Top 10 2025)
- **NIST AI RMF Integration** – Mapping to Govern, Map, Measure, Manage functions
- **Red-Teaming & Security Audit Templates** – Adversarial robustness testing, explainability assessment, human oversight verification
- **Generative AI Security Focus** – Prompt leakage prevention, adversarial attacks, system prompt safeguards
- Markdown, PDF, and CSV formats for easy use in audits and documentation

## Why Use This?
With full **EU AI Act** enforcement in 2026, non-compliance risks fines up to €35M. These checklists help you:
- Classify AI systems quickly
- Identify and mitigate **OWASP LLM vulnerabilities**
- Prepare audit-ready documentation
- Align with **NIST AI RMF** best practices
- Perform cost-effective **generative AI security audits**

Perfect for developers, compliance officers, and AI startups building trustworthy systems.
